CALLOUT 01 //
BAR-TACK REINFORCEMENT

All eight pocket corners reinforced with 42-stitch bar-tack using Coats Epic 80 thread. Rated for cable-haul stress loads. Standard on all Rigger line garments. Applied before pocket attachment to ensure seam integrity under lateral load.

CALLOUT 02 //
DROPPED SHOULDER BLOCK

Shoulder seam positioned +3cm beyond the natural shoulder point. Prevents fabric restriction during overhead cable work and speaker panel lifting. Chest ease +4cm over standard sizing block.

CALLOUT 03 //
TRIPLE-NEEDLE HOOD SEAM

Hood join seam uses 3-needle chainstitch at 4mm pitch. Rated to 180N tensile load. Drawcord channel double-topstitched at 2mm and 6mm from edge. Metal barrel cord tips prevent fraying in wet field conditions.

CALLOUT 04 //
BELT-CLEARANCE HEM

Hem length calibrated to sit above standard rigger belt attachment point, preventing fabric from being caught in buckle systems during load-out. Accounts for 440GSM drape weight. Hem tape: 3M reflective strip, Acid Green.

CALLOUT 05 //
TRIPLE-NEEDLE SIDE SEAM

All main body seams triple-needle stitched for load-bearing integrity. Side seam rated for heavy cable haul. Flatlock finish on all interior seams — no exposed raw edges against skin during extended wear.

CALLOUT 06 //
NAPE METADATA LABEL

OCR-B 6pt monospace text block printed on interior nape panel. Content: SWL rating, fabric weight, certification code, production batch. Visible only on close inspection — the brand's quietest credential.
